Welcome to The White Horse Restaurant

One of the oldest public houses in Shepshed. Now predominately a country pub & restaurant oozing rural charm and rustic character. Being close to The National Forest in Charnwood & University Market Town of Loughborough. Our picturesque surroundings provide the perfect backdrop for savouring the hearty, seasonal pub-food on our menu, and the carefully nurtured cask ales and fine wines, spirits and cocktails gracing our bar. Book a table online and enjoy a spot by our feature fireplace with a wood burning stove.

Traditionally built, warm welcoming venue where you can enjoy a fine Restaurant & Bar experience. During the month and at special times we also have entertainment evenings from relaxing piano playing to live bands.

Facilities

In April 2015 we re-opened with an extension almost doubling the original size, using local contemporary stone giving a country air welcoming feel from the outside. Whilst inside using light oak suspended beams and a feature fireplace with wood burning stove for those cooler days inside. Our alfresco eatery offers an oak wood oven baked pizzeria, which are available throughout the day & evenings. All food is freshly prepared on site using produce, where ever possible, sourced locally within a 5 mile radius.

Accessibility

Disabled parking access and toilet facilities throughout. We will also try to accommodate any and all food allergies.
